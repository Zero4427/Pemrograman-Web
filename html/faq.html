Frequently Asked Questions

Poetik is a platform that encourages sustainable waste management by allowing users to exchange waste for rewards.

To exchange waste, navigate to the Exchange page, select the type and quantity of waste, and follow the instructions to calculate your rewards.

We currently accept plastic, metal, and paper waste. Additional types may be supported in the future.

Rewards are calculated based on the type and weight of waste. Each type has a specific point value per kilogram.
